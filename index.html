About Me

Jessica is a web developer, writer, singer based in Washington, DC where she lives with her husband and their rescue dogs, Amy & Albus. She currently works with children in DC, helping to mentor and support students through Little Lights Urban Ministries. She and her husband, Jeremiah, are in the process of recording their first album together.

In her spare time, Jessica enjoys watching documentaries about the British Royal Family, going on walks with her dogs, knitting and spending time exploring her community.
